Topic: Web Design Principles

1. Color Theory
   - Color Mood
   - Analogous or Complementary colors
   - Contrast

2. Typography
   Font-families: 
   a. Serif:
      - Old Style
      - Transitional
      - Modern
      - Slab-Serif
     
   b. San-Serif:
      - Grotesque
      - Neo-Grotesque
      - Humanist
      - Geometric
      
   - 2 font styles per design.

3. User Interface (UI) Design
   - Hierarchy
   - Layout
   - Alignment
   - White space
   - Audience 

4. User Experience (UX) Design
   - Simplicity
   - Consistency
   - Reading patterns
   - All-platform design
   - Don't trick users
